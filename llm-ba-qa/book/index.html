How to Use This Book

This book is designed for practicing Business Analysts and Quality Analysts who want to integrate LLMs into their daily work. Here's how to get the most out of it:

  1. Pick chapters relevant to your role. BAs should focus on Part 2, QAs on Part 3 — but Part 1 (Foundations) and Part 4 (Advanced Patterns) are essential for everyone. Or read it all and become the team member who bridges both disciplines.
  2. Each chapter has a companion Jupyter notebook. The notebooks contain runnable code, exercises, and templates you can adapt for your own projects.
  3. Run notebooks in Google Colab — no local setup required. Every notebook includes a "Open in Colab" link. Just click, connect, and run. No Python installation, no environment headaches.
  4. Complete the capstone projects to build your portfolio. The four capstones in Part 5 combine patterns from earlier chapters into end-to-end systems you can showcase to employers and stakeholders.
